    Great For Your Plants, Great For Your Planet

    Carpe Carpum is made here in Idaho from whole invasive European carp.

    Idaho is beautiful, we all deserve clean water and healthy plants.

    With European carp under control, native species like ducks and trout will rebound.

    Carpe Carpum is the Idaho manufacturer of liquid Hydrolysate Organic Fertilizer derived from invasive European carp.

    We are fisherman with a healthy solution to the European carp problem. We are committed to the cleanup of Idaho’s lakes and rivers and we do our part by taking Idaho’s invasive European carp and converting them to an all natural, OMRI certified Organic fertilizer.

    The job is messy, but it is great for our planet, and we are happy to do it.
